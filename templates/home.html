Welcome to RGUKT Health Companion

RGUKT Health Companion is a web application designed to help people manage minor health issues like fever, cold, headache, stomach pain, and pimples. By entering their symptoms, users receive disease predictions, suitable medicine recommendations, precautions, and personalized diet and workout tips. This system promotes safe self-care and aims to reduce unnecessary hospital visits by offering trusted health guidance.
